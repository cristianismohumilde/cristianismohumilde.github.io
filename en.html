Our Mission

Integrity in the Scriptures

This movement seeks to rescue Christian truth, which is often vilified and distorted in the modern world. In Brazil, we observe the Gospel being used corruptly for political purposes or to promote shady interests.

We know that in some countries the message of God's Word is forbidden, and in others, when allowed, it is adulterated. Our mission is to bring the clarity of the original Scriptures to light.

Our Pillars

Biblical Faithfulness

Commitment to translations closest to the originals, seeking the essence of the divine message.

Christian Humility

Recognizing our smallness before the greatness of God, serving with love and without spiritual arrogance.

Resistance to Distortion

Standing firm against the use of faith for political manipulation or justification of criminality.

The Scriptures

We provide translations and studies of the main Holy Scriptures, focused on textual faithfulness.
